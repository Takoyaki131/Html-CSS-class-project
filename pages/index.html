What is Trusting the Process?

Trust the Process is a key slogan used in the NBA. More specifically in the city of Philadelphia. Coined during a rought patch for a sports team, it signifies that the fans believe in the rebuilding process of losing on purpose (otherwise known as tanking) to gain an advantage in the draft .
