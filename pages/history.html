History of the Process

2013-2014 Season (19-63)

  • Draft Michael Carter Williams 13th overall in the 2013 draft
  • July 12th - Trade Jrue Holiday to the Pelicans for 6th overall pick Nerlens Noel and Elfrid Payton
  • August 14th - Brett Brown is hired as head Coach

2014-2015 Season (18-64)

  • Draft Joel Embiid 3rd overall in the 2014 draft
  • Trade Elfrid Payton to the Orlando Magic for Dario Saric
  • Trade Thaddeus Young to the Timberwolves for the Cavaliers 2016 1st Round Pick

2015-16 Season (10-72)

  • Same Hinkie resigns as general manager
  • Bryan Calangelo is hired as new GM
  • Draft Jahlil Okafor 3rd overall and Richaun Holmes 37th overall in the 2015 draft

2016-17 Season (28-54)

  • Draft Ben Simmons 1st overall in the 2016 draft
  • Joel Embiid makes his NBA debut

2017-18 Season (52-30)

  • Draft Markelle Fultz 1st overall in the 2017 draft
  • Ben Simmons makes his NBA debut
  • Joel Embiid makes his first All-Star appearance
  • 76ers make the playoffs for the first time since 2011
  • Ben Simmons wins rookie of the year over Donovan Mitchell

2018-19 Season (51-31)

  • Trade Dario Saric, Robert Covington and Jerryd Bayless to the Minnesota Timberwolves for Jimmy Butler and Justin Patton
  • Trade Landry Shamet, Wilson Chandler and Mike Muscala to the Clippers for Tobias Harris, Boban Marjanovic and Mike Scott
  • Trade Markell Fultz to the Orlando Magic
  • Lose to the Raptors in game 7 of the Conference Finals

2019-20 Season (43-30)

  • Trade 2nd round picks to the Boston Celtics for Matisse Thybulle
  • Trade Jimmy Butler in a 4 team trade to the Miami heat
  • Lose in the 1st round to the Boston Celtics
  • Brett Brown is fired as the head coach
